## Credits

I have been a devoted fan of The Ur-Quan Masters since the 1990s, and the game gave me and my friends unforgettable memories. BattleControl is a tribute to that experience.

BattleControl is a fan-made open source project inspired by The Ur-Quan Masters and the UQM HD MegaMod.

This project would not exist without the work of the creators, contributors, maintainers, artists, writers, musicians, testers, and modders behind:

- The Ur-Quan Masters
- The Ur-Quan Masters HD MegaMod

Thank you for keeping this universe alive and for making it possible for new projects like this one to exist.

## Licensing Notes

BattleControl contains original code as well as material inspired by, derived from, or built to work with assets and ideas from The Ur-Quan Masters and the UQM HD MegaMod.

The Ur-Quan Masters project distinguishes between code and content:

- UQM code: GPLv2
- UQM content such as voice, dialogue, graphics, sounds, and music: CC BY-NC-SA 2.5

That means, in practical terms:

- attribution is required
- commercial use is not allowed for that content
- modified or derived content must remain under the same license terms

Please refer to the original upstream projects for the authoritative license texts and attribution details.
